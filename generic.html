ABOUT ME

Data & Analytics professional with over 7 years of experience in providing data-driven solutions to business problems across Customer Experience, Audit, Credit Risk, Operations, Finance, Marketing, and Human Capital in a Universal Bank rated by Fitch and Moody’s.

Equipped with over 3 years of experience in People Management as well as extensive knowledge of Analytical tools and ability to quickly adopt new technologies and skills.

I hold a Bachelors' degree in Mathematics & Statistics(2012) from the University of Lagos, an Azure Data Science Certificate (DP-100), and currently pursuing my Masters of Management Analytics (MMA) - Hybrid Learning program in Smith School of Business at Queen's University.

In my current role, I lead projects and conduct hands-on analytics tasks. Some of the projects range from credit scoring, default prediction-early warning mechanism, anomaly detection on ABM transactions, user segmentation and product/feature recommendation. The Machine learning capabilities employed in this projects are; Logistic Regression, Survival Analysis, K-Means, Apriori.

Recently, I led a 12-person team consisting of Data Engineers, Data Scientists and Analysts to develop a suite of digital products – Journey & Spend Analytics, User Segmentation, Feature Recommendation, Application Performance & Sentimental Analysis for 4 digital banking applications. In addition to the project scoping, planning, and task management, Stakeholder relations – Business teams and Management was performed.

When I am not working or studying, I engage in Yoga as a form of physical exercise. I also enjoy taking long walks, watching movies and listening to music.
